    Authentic Fall Out Boy Merchandise 100% Cotton Neon Wave Framed Picture Design Black Fall Out Boy Shirt Fall Out Boy emerged at the turn of the 21st century and has sold more than 7.5 million albums worldwide. The Chicago quartet consisting of Joe Trohman, Pete Wentz, Patrick Stump, and Andy Hurley were no strangers to the hardcore punk scene of the late ’90s. The group’s debut album, Take This to Your Grave, became an underground success and was later considered a mainstay of the pop-punk genre. In the spring of 2005, their breakout album, From Under the Cork Tree, broke into Billboards Top Ten and produced two Top Ten hits with the tracks “Sugar We’re Going Down” and “Dance Dance”. Taking an open-ended hiatus in 2009, members of the band branched out to pursue various side projects only to reunite in 2013 and release their fifth studio album, Save Rock and Roll. With the resurrection of the kings of pop-punk, Fall Out Boy continues their quest to reinvent their musical style and approach.
